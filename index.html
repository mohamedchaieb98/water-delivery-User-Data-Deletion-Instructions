User Data Deletion Instructions

App Name: Water Delivery App

Effective Date: July 6, 2025

To request deletion of your personal data from our systems, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to water.delivery.chb@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My Data
  3. Include your full name and the phone number or email you used to register

Once we receive your request, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 48 hours.

If you signed in using Facebook, you can also revoke access from your Facebook settings.

Thank you for using Water Delivery!
